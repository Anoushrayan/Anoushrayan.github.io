Recipe #1 - Fried Chicken Cutlet

  • Time: Minimum: 15-20 mins; For best quality: 4 and a half hrs
  • Serves: Depends upon quantity of ingredients
Ingredients Quantity
Minced Chicken 300 grams(3 people)
Breadcrumbs 100 grams(3 people)
Egg yolk 2 eggs(3 people)

Step 1: Form the minced chicken into a tightly packed ball or other preferred shape.

Step 2: Mix the egg yolks in a bowl to form a thick and sticky liquid.

Step 3: Take the minced chicken balls and cover with egg yolk.

Step 4: Cover the soaked minced chicken balls with breadcrumbs.

Step 5 (optional): Put breadcrumb covered minced chicken balls into refrigerator for
                              about 4 hours.

Step 6: Deep fry minced chicken balls.

                                                          Enjoy!

Recipe #2 - Soupy Pasta

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: Depends upon quantity of ingredients
Ingredients Quantity
Raw pasta 100 grams(2 people)
Any soup powder 1 packet(2 people)
Water 1 litre (approx)(2 people)

Step 1: Put pasta and soup powder in pressure cooker.

Step 2: Add water and mix soup powder.

Step 3: Pressure cook on high flame for 1 whistle and then on medium flame for 1 whistle.

                                                          Enjoy!

Recipe #3 - Eggadu

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: Depends upon quantity of ingredients
Ingredients Quantity
Maida 100 grams(2 people)
Unboiled egg 1(2 people)
Salt and spices to taste

Step 1: Blend all ingredients together.

Step 2: Put mixture in round moulds.

Step 3: Microwave for 1 min. Turn over and microwave for another minute.

                                                          Enjoy!
